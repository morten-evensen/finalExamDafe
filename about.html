About us

StayFull is a company that wants to help end the hunger crisis that are found around the world.

According to the UN, 135 million people suffer from acute hunger. This is mainly due to man-made conflicts, climate change and economic downturns. The covid-19 pandemic could now double that number.

Our sollution is the patented formula StayFull that contains all of the nutrients, vitamins and calories a person need. The StayFull powder can easilly be mixed with water to create a complete meal.


Our vision is to help the world get rid of all hunger! We envision a world where everyone has acces to the most nescessary that most of us living in the west take for granted.
